Synagoge la hriba:

Erriadh is the old Hara Seghira. This village was formerly only peopled with Jews looking, according to the local tradition, for a shelter in Djerba after the destruction in Jerusalem in the VI th century BC of Salomon Temple. Today, in this peaceful village close to La Ghriba, Jews and Muslims are living near each other with a mutual respect...
